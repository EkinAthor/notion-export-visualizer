## Overview

On the desert planet Arrakis, young Paul Atreides becomes entangled in a millennia-spanning political conspiracy centered on the most valuable substance in the universe: the spice melange. Herbert created an intricate ecology, religion, and political system that remains unmatched in science fiction.

## Memorable Quotes
