## Overview

Arthur Dent is rescued from Earth's demolition by his friend Ford Prefect, who turns out to be an alien researcher for the Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy. What follows is a wildly absurd journey through space involving depressed robots, improbability drives, and the ultimate answer to life, the universe, and everything.
