Author: Andy Weir
Status: Want to Read
Genre: Sci-Fi, Adventure
Published: November 4, 2021
Goodreads: https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/54493401.Project_Hail_Mary

## Overview

A lone astronaut wakes up on a spaceship with no memory of how he got there. He gradually pieces together that he's humanity's last hope, sent on a desperate mission to save Earth from an extinction-level threat. Known for its blend of hard science, humor, and an unforgettable alien friendship.

## Why It's On My List

- Loved *The Martian* by the same author
- Multiple friends have called it their favorite book of the decade
- The science is reportedly accurate and accessible
- The alien character Rocky is apparently one of the best in sci-fi
