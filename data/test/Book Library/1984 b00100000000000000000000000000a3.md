## Overview

Winston Smith lives under the totalitarian regime of the Party in Oceania, where Big Brother watches everything and the Thought Police punish dissent. His quiet rebellion through forbidden love and secret diary-keeping leads him into the depths of the Party's machinery of control.

## Memorable Quotes

> "War is peace. Freedom is slavery. Ignorance is strength."

> "Who controls the past controls the future. Who controls the present controls the past."

## Themes

- Totalitarianism and surveillance
- The manipulation of truth and history
- The destruction of individual thought
- Language as a tool of control
