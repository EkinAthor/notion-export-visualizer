# The Midnight Library

Author: Matt Haig
Status: Want to Read
Genre: Fiction, Fantasy, Philosophy
Published: September 29, 2020
Goodreads: https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/52578297.The_Midnight_Library

## Overview

Between life and death there is a library where each book represents a different life you could have lived. Nora Seed finds herself in this library and must decide what truly makes life worth living. A thought-provoking exploration of regret, possibility, and the choices that shape us.

## Themes

- Regret and second chances
- The meaning of a good life
- Parallel lives and the multiverse of choices
- Depression and hope
