## Overview

Set in the Jazz Age on Long Island, this slim novel packs enormous thematic weight into its pages. Through the eyes of Nick Carraway, we witness the rise and fall of the mysterious Jay Gatsby and his obsessive pursuit of Daisy Buchanan.

## Memorable Quotes

> "So we beat on, boats against the current, borne back ceaselessly into the past."

> "I hope she'll be a fool — that's the best thing a girl can be in this world, a beautiful little fool."

## Themes

- The corruption of the American Dream
- The hollowness of the upper class
- The impossibility of recapturing the past
- Appearance vs. reality
